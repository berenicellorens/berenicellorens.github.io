radio

Over the past three years, I have been exploring curation and radio production. My first experience was with AMPLIFY RADIO x REA, a show born from my residency at Radio Electrónica Argentina as part of the AMPLIFY DAI. Currently, I am working on Beyond of_line, a show broadcast at Refuge Worldwide, where i share not only music but also occasionally creating radio art.
