Sound Works and IA + Music in LAtam Report // A veces con pena, a veces con esperanza // COllab. with Futurx-LARROSA-Amplify DAI 2023

A veces con penas, a veces con esperanzas", a collaborative sound work composed by Berenice Llorens, Pol Lain and Maia Koenig created in the framework of the IA+MUSIC LATAM research and reporting by FuturX together with Larrosa.pro. The artists of @amplify.dai immersed themselves together with Futurx and Larrosa in an investigation and experimentation of AI tools for music generation, voice, text and image cloning, conceptualising their use through dialogue and interactive work. From this process emerges this experimental and abstract sound work where a collage of textures, vocal traces and folkloric rhythms generated from an AI accompany a copla sung by the new "VOZ CANTORA LATAM" created from cloning and which brings together and enhances the power and art of many of our Latin American singer-songwriters in a single voice. --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Live A/V // Portal del Sur // COllab. with Carol Santana (Brasil) 2021/22

Portal del Sur is the works made with the artist Carol Santana (Brasil) during the Artlab Artistic Residencies program by Amplify D.A.I. -Útero, Terra, Ventre, Reptilia, Raiz, Del sur, Sur e, Matriz, Portais, Origem, Portal de sur, Earth, Portal, Connection, Mother earth, Source, Nature, Ancestrality, Climate crisis- Portal del Sur is memory, listening, remembrance, visibility, connection, nature, origin, transformation, processes and technology. It is a call to enter a sonic and visual world in which the ancestral culture of Latin America, its customs and part of its ecosystem become visible and resonate, inviting the listener to be part of a sensory journey created by different natural and artificial landscapes. As its name suggests, it is the gateway to the interplay of three creative processes: the painting of indigenous artist Yaka Sales, the real-time visuals mixed with analog by Carol Santana and the sound atmospheres created by Berenice Llorens. Traversing different cultures, this collaboration resonates on a common axis: -the connection with our origin, the connection of humans with their environment, and of life with technology-. Through images, the different materials within Portal del Sur and their processes and/or transformation, bring Latin American culture to the forefront, and its sound a vehicle of one of today's most pressing issues: the climate crisis and deforestation. -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- AMPLIFY D.A.I is an initiative of the British Council in partnership with MUTEK Montréal, MUTEK Buenos Aires and Somerset House Studios in the UK. The programme is supported by Canada Council for the Arts and Fundación Williams.

< LIVE FROM THE MULTIVERSE (album) // Collab as a Guitar Player with The Sonic Arts Ensemble Ohio, Usa (2022)

Ravello Records and The Sonic Arts Ensemble present LIVE FROM THE MULTIVERSE, an album leveraging the possibilities of online performance and expanding the boundaries of contemporary music. Musicians from three different continents connect through the internet on low latency, high quality audio networks to perform repertoire ranging from composed, notated scores to freely structured pieces born from masterfully executed ensemble improvisation. With the utilization of extended techniques, singing bowls, and imaginative sound design, the ensemble develops and thrives in an enticing and innovative soundscape.// Review by Take Effect: “A Rising Tide Of Light”, the best track, lands in the middle, and recruits Norah Zuniga-Shaw’s voice, James Croson’s keys, and Berenice Llorens’ guitar for a very dreamy, psychedelic exploration of chamber prog-rock ideas, while “Octet/Sextet”makes great use of Joseph Sferra’s clarinet, Samuel Gardner’s keys and Ainger’s laptop manipulation into a very distinct electronic influenced landscape.

A/V // Respiration // Marmotas Dreams & Kardaver 2021/22

Respiration es un viaje de voces e interpretación de poemas de versos libre, bases electrónicas, procesos digitales y analógicos que dialogan con un texto visual realizado por @kardaver usando programación y softwares libres. A/V // Cascadas (2020/2021) // Collab. with Jimena Aldana

Cascadas (2020/2021) es una pieza audiovisual de Berenice Llorens (música) y Jimena Aldana (visuales). En la obra, un protagonista no humano, se va transformando y reconstruyendo a sí mismo sobre melodías flotantes inmersa en colchones sonoros generados por sonidos y ruidos que imitan la contaminación auditiva del contexto actual. Esta creada con collages manuales y digitales que captan de lo sonoro, paisajes naturales en equilibrio y/o desequilibrio de climas más robóticos.
