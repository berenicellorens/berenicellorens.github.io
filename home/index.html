berenice/lorens

My practice is a mixture of compositions, performances, radio, sound art, and research. I work with sounds, writings, and drawings, exploring different ways of listening to and perceiving our surroundings to reflect on our relationship with the environment.

I record, recycle, and reinterpret sounds from nature, the city, and machines to create evolving sonic ecosystems. My research delves into the transformation of sound and its relationship to time, memory, and adaptation across different territories—allowing me to better understand how we listen to, connect with, and modify the spaces we inhabit.

--it is a process in constant transformation--
