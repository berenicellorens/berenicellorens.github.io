The connection with the environment we inhabit—how we are transformed by its perception and how we influence it—is central to my art. Through music, words, and drawings, I invite reflection on how we listen and see, engaging in an exercise of bringing the outside in and the inside out, provoking a deeper awareness of our relationship with the environment.
IT IS A PROCESS IN CONSTANT TRANSFORMATION.
