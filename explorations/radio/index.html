radio

Over the past three years, I have been focusing on curating and producing radio programs. My first experience was with RADIO AMPLIFY x REA, a show born out of my residency at Radio Electrónica Argentina as part of the AMPLIFY DAI program. Currently, I am working on Beyond of_line, a monthly program broadcast on Refuge Worldwide, where I explore the expansion of experimental electronic music and sound art, sharing not only music but also creating radio art in certain instances.
