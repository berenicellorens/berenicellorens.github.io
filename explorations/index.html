explorations

Besides sound, I explore drawings, writings, and video art. It serves as a kind of daily blog where I create projects such as Rayaduras, Blabladurías de Berenice, and La Cursividad. Rayaduras is the name I give to my drawing and painting collection, created over the past 20 years. Alongside my musical studies, I also trained in graphic design and video editing because I have always been interested in expanding the ways we perceive and reinterpret our surroundings. Since childhood, I have written poems and later ventured into short stories and other writings. I named my first writing project La Cursividad. This term is a play on words; it represents an unbroken flow of emotions that glides like continuous handwriting—a surging overflow of feelings, sometimes exaggerated. It embodies a way of writing where "cursi" translates to "corny" in English, and "cursiva" is expressed as "italic". After a pause of nearly three years, in 2020, I created blabladurias de berenice, which includes the "escritos instantáneos" collection. Through spontaneous scenes and listening experiences, I capture moments from my life in words, akin to a scream or a photograph. You can also visit the "La Cursividad" section, a blog where most of my writings and poems are featured.
