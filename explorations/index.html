explorations

Besides sound, and as part of an interdisciplinary approach to my work, I explore drawing, writing, poetry, and video art. These practices nurture and expand my sonic explorations, functioning as a kind of daily blog where I develop projects such as Rayaduras, Blabladurías de Berenice, and La Cursividad. I create spontaneous writings and drawings as a way of listening to the environment and observing how it shapes my thoughts.
